Artima offers volume discounts on both eBooks and paper books. A volume discount will be applied automatically when you place 10 or more copies of an item in your shopping cart.

Volume discounts on eBooks

If you purchase multiple copies of an eBook, you will receive one download URL for each copy purchased. You can distribute those URLs to others, who can follow the URL to "claim" them. Each eBook will be licensed to, and personalized for, the person who claims it.

  • Copies 10 to 19, 30% discount
  • Copies 20 to 29, 35% discount
  • Copies 30 to 39, 40% discount
  • Copies 40 to 49, 45% discount
  • Copies 50 and up, 50% discount
